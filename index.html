Progression

Five levels.
No ceiling on ambition.

Every pattern follows the same progression architecture — from foundational control to elite athletic expression. You know exactly where you are and exactly what comes next.

0
Pattern Isolation
Corrective work. Building the neuromuscular foundation before adding load.
Entry point
1
Bilateral Control
Competent two-limb movement under light-moderate load with good mechanics.
e.g. Goblet Squat 32kg
2
Bilateral Strength
Loading the pattern under meaningful resistance with consistent technique.
e.g. Back Squat 1×BW
3
Unilateral Control
Progressing to single-limb work — the real test of movement quality under fatigue.
e.g. Bulgarian Split Squat
4
Unilateral Strength
Heavy single-limb loading. Few people reach this. It requires real work to get here.
e.g. Single-leg Deadlift
5
Unilateral Athletic Expression
Elite-level movement integration. Force production, velocity, and precision combined.
